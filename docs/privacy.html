Privacy Policy

Destiny Lines · Effective July 6, 2026

Destiny Lines ("we", "us") is an AI-powered palm reading app for entertainment purposes. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

Information We Collect

  • Account information. Your email address and a hashed password, used to create and secure your account.
  • Palm photos. When you request a reading, the photo you take or select is transmitted securely to our servers and to our AI provider for analysis. Palm photos are processed in memory and are never stored — not on our servers and not in your reading history.
  • Readings. The text of your palm readings is stored in your account so you can revisit them in the History tab.
  • Usage counts. We track how many readings you have used to enforce your plan's limits.
  • Subscription status. If you subscribe, purchases are processed by Apple and managed through RevenueCat. We receive your subscription tier and expiration date, never your payment details.

How Palm Photos Are Processed

Palm images are analyzed by an AI vision model operated by our AI provider (OpenAI). The image is sent for the sole purpose of generating your reading and is subject to the provider's API data-usage policies, under which API data is not used to train their models. We do not retain the image after your reading is generated.

How We Use Information

  • To provide palm readings and maintain your reading history
  • To manage your subscription and enforce plan limits
  • To respond to support requests

We do not sell your personal information, and we do not use it for third-party advertising.

Third-Party Services

  • Supabase — authentication, database, and secure processing infrastructure
  • OpenAI — AI analysis of palm images
  • Apple App Store & RevenueCat — subscription billing and management

Data Retention & Deletion

Your account data and readings are retained until you delete your account. You can permanently delete your account and all associated data at any time from Profile → Delete Account inside the app. Deletion is immediate and irreversible.

Age Requirement

Destiny Lines is intended for users aged 16 and older. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Security

Data is transmitted over encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS) and stored with access controls that restrict each account's data to that account.

Your Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, export, or delete your personal information. Most of these are available directly in the app; for anything else, contact us and we will respond promptly.

Changes

If we make material changes to this policy, we will update this page and the effective date above.

Contact

Questions or requests: support@destinylines.app
